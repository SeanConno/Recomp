About Recomp

Built for a personalised 20-week body recomposition plan combining Hyrox training, half marathon preparation, 14,000+ daily steps and structured nutrition. Data is saved locally in your browser and persists between sessions.

Complete the weekly check-in every Sunday evening. Update the current week in Settings every Monday. The Week Ahead plan auto-adjusts macros based on last week's BF% delta vs schedule.
