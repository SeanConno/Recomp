About Recomp v8

20-week body recomposition plan for Hyrox athletes. Enter a plan start date — all dates auto-adjust. Current week calculates from today automatically. Dynamic macro engine uses Mifflin-St Jeor BMR × activity multiplier, adjusting weekly as you log weight. Training Readiness from Garmin gates session intensity and flex day eligibility. Data stores locally in your browser.
